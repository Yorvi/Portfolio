WHO AM I

Hello there, lets break the ice a little shall we.

    My name is Yorvin Rosario, and I'm based in New York, NY. I was born in the Dominican Republic and raised in New York ever since the age of three. Early on in life video games and computers took my attention away from anything else. I loved being able to play around and customize my experience any way possible. My love for technology led me to consider a career in coding. I started to learn the basics of coding through YouTube videos and some free courses online before enrolling into a bootcamp to hopefully improve my newfound skills. Three months later I graduated from the New York Code + Design Academy, a wonderful bootcamp that taught me Ruby on Rails web development. I specialize in front-end, UI & UX, back-end, and responsive design.

Enough of the boring backstory here are some interesting facts about me.

  1. Although I was raised in New York for most of my entire life, I am still a die hard Red Sox and Celtics Fan.
  2. My favorite animes in order are Naruto, Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood, & Hunter x Hunter.
  3. Eventhough I code, I don't really like coffee.
  4. I used to code on my calculator before I even knew how to code.
  5. Eventhough my name is spelt Yorvin, I grew up thinking it was Jorvin up until I graduated High School.
